Game Instructions:

  • Use arrow keys (↑ ↓ ← →) to move. Diagonal movement is supported.
  • Shoot using the configured key (space or any chosen letter)
  • Earn points based on the enemy row:
    • Bottom row (4th): 5 points
    • 3rd row: 10 points
    • 2nd row: 15 points
    • Top row (1st): 20 points
  • You have 3 lives — getting hit 3 times ends the game
  • Enemy ships speed up every 5 seconds (up to 4 times)
  • You can only move in the bottom 40% of the screen
  • Reach the time limit or destroy all enemies to win
